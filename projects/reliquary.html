Reliquary is a collection of relics for Slay the Spire. You can download it here, and take a gander at the source code on Github. The latest version is 0.1 (2021/1/24).

Relics

Boiling Flask
Uncommon
At the start of each combat, if your potion slots are full, choose a Vapor card and add it into your hand.
(Vapors are cards with minor effects derived from your potions. They Retain and Exhaust.)
Bookmark
Uncommon
At the start of each combat, draw an extra card for each Innate card in your deck.
Boomerang
Common
Put the first card you play each turn on top of your draw pile.
Broken Clock
Shop
Each combat starts with two player turns, then two enemy turns.
Ember
Boss
Gain at the start of your turn. Take 1 damage for each beyond the third you spend each turn.
Expired Coupon
Common
Whenever you reach 80 or more total unblocked damage in a combat, deals 10 damage to ALL enemies.
Feather Duster
Uncommon
Upon pickup, remove any number of cards from your deck except Strike, Defend, and Curse cards.
Firecrackers
Uncommon
Overkill damage is dealt to ALL other enemies.
Iridium Chain
Uncommon
Whenever you play a Power card, put an Ethereal copy into your hand that costs 1 more.
Jackalope Antler
Uncommon
Whenever an enemy dies, refund the Energy cost of the last card you played.
Kinked Spring
Boss
Gain at the start of your turn. Your discard pile can't shuffle into your draw pile during your turn.
Knoch
Common
Enemies with 25% or less HP are Weak.
Medicine Ball
Uncommon · Ironclad
You have additional Strength equal to your enemies' combined Strength.
Mudwin's Cradle
Uncommon · Watcher
Whenever you exit Divinity, gain 2 Mantra for each time you entered Divinity this combat (up to a max of 9).
Ouija Board
Rare
Once each turn, right click to play a card from your discard pile with random targets and shuffle it into your draw pile. It costs 1 more.
Porcupine Quills
Common
Whenever you receive 12 or more unblocked damage, deal that much damage back.
Purple-Tinged Leaf
Uncommon
Upon pickup, choose a card. Whenever you play that card, heal 1 HP.
Quartz Cube
Uncommon
At the start of each combat with 2 or more enemies, apply 2 Stunned to one. It can't be damaged while Stunned.
Radioactive Pellet
Common · Silent
Poison doesn't decrement.
Red Paperclip
Boss
Gain at the start of your turn. Replaces a random Uncommon relic.
Rose-Tinted Glasses
Common
The first 2 times you draw a Status each combat, Exhaust it and draw a card.
Rosewood Lute
Common
Whenever you block damage exactly, gain next turn.
Sculpting Steel
Uncommon
Upon pickup, becomes a copy of a random relic you already have.
Short Fuse
Uncommon · Defect
Your Lightning Orbs target the enemy with the least HP.
Silk Glove
Common
Whenever you would discard a single card at the end of your turn, Retain it.
Stiletto
Common
Your Attacks deal double damage to enemies' Block.
Tattered Rug
Common
You can sell potions to the Merchant.
Trident Head
Uncommon
The first card you play on a single enemy each combat is copied to all other enemies.
Writ of Mandamus
Boss
Gain at the start of your turn. At the end of your turn, you lose 3 Block.
