Reliquary is a collection of relics for Slay the Spire. You find it on the Steam Workshop, and take a gander at the source code on Github. The latest version is 0.1 (changelog).

Relics

Aerogel
Uncommon
Upon pickup, choose a card. Whenever you shuffle your draw pile with this card in it, it will end up on top.
Ball Bearing
Uncommon
Whenever you shuffle your draw pile, shuffle in a Flash of Steel or Finesse, chosen randomly.
Boiling Flask
Uncommon
At the start of each combat, if your potion slots are full, choose a Vapor card and add it into your hand.
(Vapors are cards with minor effects derived from your potions. They Retain and Exhaust.)
Bookmark
Uncommon
At the start of each combat, draw an extra card for each Innate card in your deck.
Boomerang
Common
Put the first card you play each turn on top of your draw pile.
Broken Clock
Shop
Each combat starts with two player turns, then two enemy turns.
Buckler
Common
The next 2 times you play an upgradeable card that's in your deck, Upgrade it permanently.
Ember
Boss
Gain at the start of your turn. Take 1 damage for each beyond the third you spend each turn.
Expired Coupon
Common
Whenever you reach 80 or more total unblocked damage dealt in a combat, deal 10 damage to ALL enemies.
Feather Duster
Uncommon
Upon pickup, remove any number of cards from your deck except Strike, Defend, and Curse cards.
Firecrackers
Uncommon
Overkill damage is dealt to a random enemy.
Hot Poker
Uncommon
Whenever you draw a card during your turn, deal damage equal to its cost to a random enemy.
Iridium Chain
Uncommon
Whenever you play a Power card, put an Ethereal copy into your hand that costs 1 more.
Ivory Trinket
Common · Watcher
Every 10th time you Retain a Miracle, add a Miracle into your hand.
Jackalope Antler
Uncommon
Whenever an enemy dies, refund the Energy cost of the last card you played.
Kill Pill
Rare · Silent
Whenever you apply Poison, it triggers immediately.
Kinked Spring
Boss
Gain at the start of your turn. Your discard pile can't shuffle into your draw pile during your turn.
Knoch
Common
Enemies with 25% or less HP are Weak.
Love-Emitting Diode
Uncommon · Defect
Positive Focus triply affects your rightmost Orb.
Medicine Ball
Uncommon · Ironclad
You have additional Strength equal to your enemies' combined Strength.
Mudwin's Cradle
Uncommon · Watcher
Whenever you exit Divinity, gain 2 Mantra for each time you entered Divinity this combat (up to a max of 9).
Ouija Board
Rare
Once each turn, play a card from your discard pile with random targets and shuffle it into your draw pile. It costs 1 more.
(Right click to activate.)
Party Balloon
Common
Power cards start each combat in the top half of your draw pile.
Porcupine Quills
Common
Whenever you receive 12 or more unblocked damage, deal that much damage back.
Purple-Tinged Leaf
Uncommon
Upon pickup, choose a card. Whenever you play this card, heal 1 HP.
Quartz Cube
Uncommon
At the start of each combat with 2 or more enemies, apply 2 Stunned to one. It can't be damaged while Stunned.
Radioactive Pellet
Common · Silent
Poison doesn't decrement.
Red Cape
Boss
Gain at the start of your turn. Whenever you Attack a single enemy, other enemies can't be targeted until it dies.
Red Paperclip
Boss
Gain at the start of your turn. Replaces a random Uncommon relic.
Rose-Tinted Glasses
Common
The first 2 times you draw a Status each combat, Exhaust it and draw a card.
Rosewood Lute
Common
Whenever you block damage exactly, gain next turn.
Sculpting Steel
Uncommon
Upon pickup, becomes a copy of a random relic you already have.
Sharkskin Sheath
Common
Whenever you end your turn with no Energy or cards in hand, gain and draw a card next turn.
Short Fuse
Rare · Defect
Your Lightning Orbs target the enemy with the least HP.
Silk Glove
Common
Whenever you would discard a single card at the end of your turn, Retain it.
Stiletto
Common
Enemies gain 50% less Block.
Tam-Tam
Rare · Ironclad
Whenever you completely block an attack, gain 2 Strength and ALL enemies gain 1 Strength.
Tattered Rug
Common
You can sell potions to the Merchant.
Trident Head
Uncommon
The first card you play on a single enemy each combat is copied to all other enemies.
Tuning Fork
Common
Whenever you break an enemy's Block exactly, deal 10 damage to it.
Writ of Mandamus
Boss
Gain at the start of your turn. At the end of your turn, you lose 3 Block.
